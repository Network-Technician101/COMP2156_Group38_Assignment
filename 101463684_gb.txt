George Brown College (GBC) is a public college in Toronto, Ontario, Canada, offering a wide range of programs in applied arts, business, health sciences, community services, and more.
GBC offers various student services such as academic advising, career services, counseling, and mentorship programs.
Programs: GBC offers programs in areas such as business, health sciences, community services, technology, hospitality, arts, and design. The college has both full-time and part-time options.
Degrees & Diplomas: The college provides certificate programs, diplomas, and degrees (in partnership with universities). Notably, GBC offers degree programs in areas like early childhood education, nursing, and fashion.
Location: Toronto, Ontario, Canada
